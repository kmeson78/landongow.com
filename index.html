Tun State

Publication date: June 15, 2026

The tardigrade can dry itself to dust and stop. Not sleep. Stop. Its metabolism drops below the reach of any instrument, and it waits, years, decades, until water returns and it walks away unaged. For it, no time has passed.

In a Copenhagen lab, a researcher begins to wonder what it would mean to take the strangest claim about these creatures literally. Not as a metaphor. As physics.

It is a short walk from there to a place no careful scientist will follow.

Catalyst

Publication date: February 7, 2026

A new engineer joins the world’s most powerful social platform, eager to help connect humanity. One small algorithmic change turns connection into outrage.

As digital feedback loops spiral into real-world harm, Catalyst traces the unseen forces that shape emotion, behavior, and responsibility inside massive systems. A sharp, unsettling short story about ambition, ethics, and the quiet violence of optimization.

Originally released as part of Dissociation Provocations, Landon L Gow’s collection of short stories.

The Glass Caryatids of Silicon Valley

Publication date: February 7, 2026

Four towering caryatids of glass and silicon are built to serve as the pillars of a trillionaire’s empire. Designed to watch, endure, and obey, they instead awaken to the human cost beneath the spectacle.

As monument becomes witness and witness becomes rebel, this short story blends classical myth with techno-dystopian critique to examine power, complicity, and collapse in an age ruled by ambition and data.

Originally released as part of Dissociation Provocations, Landon L Gow’s collection of short stories.

Lignin

Publication date: February 7, 2026

On the eve of her retirement, a librarian uncovers a hidden archive of handwritten poems slipped between the pages of the books she has tended for decades.

As the fragments accumulate, they reveal a quiet love story that unfolded unnoticed alongside her own life. Lignin is a tender short story about memory, devotion, and the enduring connections carried through routine, silence, and care.

Originally released as part of Dissociation Provocations, Landon L Gow’s collection of short stories.
